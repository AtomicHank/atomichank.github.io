143rd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 143rd Infantry Regiment represents one of the Army's carried-forward regimental lineages, linking present-day heraldry and honors to earlier citizen-soldier formations.

Its identity was maintained through successive redesignations and changes in structure, a common pattern among regiments preserved in the modern order of battle primarily through lineage.

The 143rd Infantry continues to illustrate the Army's effort to preserve unit memory across changing eras.
