134th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 134th Infantry Regiment was preserved within the Army's modern regimental lineage system as part of its broader structure of mobilization and continuity. It represents the institutional side of Army history more than an uninterrupted independent regimental field narrative.

Through lineage and honors, the regiment's identity endured as the Army moved toward battalion and brigade organization.

The 134th Infantry remains part of the service's wider modern heritage.
