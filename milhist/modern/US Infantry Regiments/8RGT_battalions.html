8th Infantry Regiment Battalions

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

This subpage gathers the battalion insignia associated with the modern 8th Infantry Regiment, the Fighting Eagles. The public CMH battalion list currently documents the 1st and 2d Battalions, while the local image set also includes a 3d Battalion icon.

1st Battalion

1st Battalion, 8th Infantry Regiment

The 1st Battalion is the best-known modern combat battalion of the Fighting Eagles. It carried the regiment's lineage through the battalion era and gave the 8th Infantry its clearest Vietnam chapter under the 4th Infantry Division, extending a divisional identity already made famous on Utah Beach and in the European campaign of World War II.

2d Battalion

2d Battalion, 8th Infantry Regiment

The 2d Battalion represents the regiment's continued survival inside the modern modular Army after the decline of the traditional regiment as a field headquarters. Alongside the 1st Battalion it kept the 8th Infantry's divisional heritage alive into the later Cold War and post-Cold War force.

3d Battalion

3d Battalion, 8th Infantry Regiment

The local icon set includes a 3d Battalion graphic, but the current public CMH listing for the 8th Infantry Regiment documents only the 1st and 2d Battalions. This section therefore serves as a heritage placeholder within the icon set rather than as a fully documented public battalion lineage summary.

Research note: The 1st and 2d Battalion blurbs follow the regiment's public history and current CMH battalion listing checked on 24 March 2026. The 3d Battalion note reflects the difference between the icon set and the public battalion list.
