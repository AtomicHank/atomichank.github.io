92nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 92nd Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's set of higher-numbered modern lineages used to preserve unit identity and provide organizational depth. It reflects the broader structure that supported Army mobilization in the world-war and Cold War eras.

Like many regiments in this range, its continuity was maintained more through the official lineage system than through a single uninterrupted regimental field history. That continuity still mattered for the Army's internal organization and historical record.

The 92nd Infantry forms part of that larger institutional framework of the modern Army.
