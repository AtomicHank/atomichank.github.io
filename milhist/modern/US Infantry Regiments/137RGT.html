137th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 137th Infantry Regiment was maintained within the Army's modern lineage system as part of the broad roster of numbered infantry identities. Its importance lies in continuity of honors and organizational identity through repeated structural change.

Like many regiments in this range, its lineage outlasted any single full-regiment field career and remained part of the Army's administrative memory.

The 137th Infantry thus contributes to the Army's wider heritage of preserved lineages.
