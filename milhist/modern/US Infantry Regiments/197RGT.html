197th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 197th Infantry Regiment survives as part of the Army's preserved family of regiment lineages and maintained historical designations.

Its place in Army history was carried through lineage and honors, allowing the regiment's identity to outlast repeated structural change.

The 197th Infantry remains part of the Army's institutional memory.
