106th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 106th Infantry Regiment is part of the Army's preserved set of higher-numbered regimental lineages. It reflects the administrative depth and mobilization potential built into the modern Army.

Its identity was maintained through lineage and honors as the Army moved away from the old permanent regimental field structure and toward more flexible combined-arms formations.

The 106th Infantry therefore forms part of the larger framework of Army continuity across modern organizational change.
