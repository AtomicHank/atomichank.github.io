195th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 195th Infantry Regiment survives as one of the historical regimental identities preserved through the Army's lineage-and-honors process.

Its continued recognition reflects the Army's determination to retain names, symbols, and distinctions across generations of reorganization.

The 195th Infantry remains part of the Army's maintained regimental heritage.
