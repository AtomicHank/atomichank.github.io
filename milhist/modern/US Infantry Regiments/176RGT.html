176th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 176th Infantry Regiment is preserved within the Army's lineage system as part of the broader body of later-numbered infantry regiments.

Its history is carried chiefly through designation, honors, and administrative continuity rather than a single uninterrupted operational structure.

The 176th Infantry remains part of the Army's larger regimental heritage.
