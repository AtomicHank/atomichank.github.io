59th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 59th Infantry Regiment was one of the numbered infantry lineages associated with the Army's enlarged modern force structure. It reflects the service's need for organizational depth during the world-war and Cold War eras, even when many such regiments were not always active in the field.

Its preserved lineage shows how the Army managed expansion and contraction without discarding unit identity. In that respect, the regiment is part of the hidden institutional scaffolding behind larger mobilization efforts.

The 59th Infantry is therefore best understood as a continuity lineage within the broader development of the modern Army.
