125th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 125th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved pool of modern infantry lineages. It illustrates how unit names and honors were retained across force-structure changes even when full regiments were not continuously active in the field.

Its historical significance is therefore rooted in continuity, redesignation, and lineage preservation rather than in one uninterrupted regimental campaign record.

The 125th Infantry remains part of the Army's broader modern institutional heritage.
