162nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 162nd Infantry Regiment is retained within the Army's historical record as a preserved regimental designation.

As with many similar units, its place in Army history is carried mainly by lineage, honors, and the continuation of official identity through redesignation.

The 162nd Infantry remains part of the Army's maintained regimental tradition.
