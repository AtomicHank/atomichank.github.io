179th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 179th Infantry Regiment is retained within the Army's lineage framework as a preserved historical designation.

Its continuity illustrates how regimental identities were sustained through redesignation and inherited honors despite major structural change.

The 179th Infantry remains part of the Army's broader modern heritage.
