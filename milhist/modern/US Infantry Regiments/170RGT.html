170th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 170th Infantry Regiment is retained in Army records as one of the preserved regimental lineages carried forward into the modern period.

Its record reflects the Army's long practice of sustaining names, honors, and symbols despite repeated changes in force structure.

The 170th Infantry remains one of the regiment identities maintained in the modern era.
