155th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 155th Infantry Regiment survives as one of the Army's preserved regimental lineages, linking present-day identity to earlier volunteer and National Guard service.

As with many similar units, its continuity is reflected in lineage and honors more than in a continuously active independent regiment.

The 155th Infantry remains part of the Army's stored institutional memory.
