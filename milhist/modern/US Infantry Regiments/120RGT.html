120th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 120th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved higher-numbered modern lineages. It reflects the service's effort to retain names, honors, and identity across periods of mobilization and organizational change.

As with many regiments in this range, its continuity is chiefly visible through official lineage and honors rather than through one uninterrupted field-regiment story.

The 120th Infantry remains part of the Army's wider institutional and historical framework.
