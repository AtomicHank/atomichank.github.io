161st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 161st Infantry Regiment survives in the Army's lineage system as part of the preserved framework of later-numbered infantry regiments.

Its continuity rests on designation, honors, and heraldry, which allowed regimental identities to endure across generations of organizational change.

The 161st Infantry remains part of the larger story of modern American infantry heritage.
