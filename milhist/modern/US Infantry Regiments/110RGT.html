110th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 110th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved set of modern infantry lineages. It reflects the way the service maintained a wider range of regimental identities than its continuously active field force alone required.

Like many regiments in this range, its continuity is chiefly visible through official lineage rather than one uninterrupted combat narrative.

The 110th Infantry therefore contributes to the Army's larger institutional continuity across the modern era.
