193rd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 193rd Infantry Regiment forms part of the Army's preserved set of infantry lineages and survives as a historical designation in the modern record.

Its continuity is chiefly administrative and heraldic, reflecting preserved honors and inherited unit identity.

The 193rd Infantry remains part of the Army's broader institutional memory.
