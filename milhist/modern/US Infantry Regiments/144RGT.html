144th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 144th Infantry Regiment survives in the Army's lineage system as part of the larger preservation of American regimental history from the volunteer and National Guard tradition.

Although many such regiments did not remain continuously active in a single form, their honors, designations, and institutional memory were retained through later reorganizations.

The 144th Infantry remains part of that preserved modern heritage.
