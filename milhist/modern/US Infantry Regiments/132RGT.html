132nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 132nd Infantry Regiment is one of the preserved higher-numbered infantry lineages maintained through the Army's modern regimental system. Its history reflects continuity and institutional memory more than a continuously active regimental field life.

Like many lineages in this range, its identity persisted through honors, redesignation, and administrative continuity as the Army reorganized.

The 132nd Infantry therefore remains part of the Army's broader historical framework.
