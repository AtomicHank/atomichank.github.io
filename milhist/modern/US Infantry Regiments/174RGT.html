174th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 174th Infantry Regiment survives within Army lineage records as one of the later-numbered regimental identities preserved across time.

Its history is represented principally through honors, lineage, and administrative continuity rather than uninterrupted activity in a single form.

The 174th Infantry remains part of the Army's broader institutional memory.
