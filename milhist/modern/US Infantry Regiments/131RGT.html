131st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 131st Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved modern lineage structure. It reflects the long-term retention of unit identity across mobilization, redesignation, and changing tactical organizations.

Its continuity is visible mainly through the lineage and honors system rather than through a single uninterrupted independent regimental career.

The 131st Infantry remains part of the Army's wider institutional memory and heritage.
