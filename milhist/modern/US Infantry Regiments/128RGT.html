128th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 128th Infantry Regiment was preserved within the Army's modern regimental lineage system as part of its wider structure of continuity and mobilization depth. It reflects the institutional side of Army history more than a single uninterrupted independent field-regiment career.

Through honors, redesignation, and recorded lineage, the regiment's identity endured while the Army reorganized around newer tactical structures.

The 128th Infantry remains part of that broader preserved heritage.
