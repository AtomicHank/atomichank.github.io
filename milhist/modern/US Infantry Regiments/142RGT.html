142nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 142nd Infantry Regiment is one of the regimental identities carried forward into the Army's modern lineage system, preserving the memory of earlier volunteer and National Guard service.

Like many regiments in this numerical range, its story is defined by mobilization, reorganization, and the continued preservation of honors rather than by an uninterrupted independent regimental career.

The 142nd Infantry remains part of the Army's preserved historical structure.
