124th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 124th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's modern regimental lineage system and reflects continuity of identity through changing tactical organizations. It is one of the many lineages retained to preserve honors and structure beyond the active field force alone.

As with many comparable regiments, its modern profile is more institutional than continuously operational at full-regiment scale.

The 124th Infantry therefore forms part of the Army's preserved historical framework.
