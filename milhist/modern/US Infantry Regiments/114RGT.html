114th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 114th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved modern lineage pool and reflects the service's effort to keep unit identities alive across multiple generations of organizational change. It is part of the broader structure behind twentieth-century mobilization and continuity.

Its history survives primarily through lineage and honors rather than through a single uninterrupted independent field-regiment career.

That continuity still gives the 114th Infantry a clear place within the Army's institutional memory.
