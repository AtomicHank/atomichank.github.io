97th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 97th Infantry Regiment formed part of the Army's wider system of modern numbered regiments maintained for mobilization, flexibility, and continuity. Its preserved identity demonstrates how the Army retained organizational depth beyond the most visible active field units.

As the service moved toward battalion and brigade structures, lineages such as the 97th Infantry were often maintained through formal honors and redesignations rather than through uninterrupted regimental activity.

That makes the 97th Infantry a useful example of the Army's institutional approach to preserving history.
