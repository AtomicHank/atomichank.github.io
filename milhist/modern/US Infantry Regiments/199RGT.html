199th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 199th Infantry Regiment is retained in the Army's historical framework as one of the preserved later-numbered regiment identities.

Its history is reflected in the continuation of lineage, honors, and symbolism across redesignation and evolving force structures.

The 199th Infantry remains part of the Army's maintained historical record.
