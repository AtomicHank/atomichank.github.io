198th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 198th Infantry Regiment forms part of the Army's lineage-and-honors structure, preserving a regimental identity from earlier periods of service.

As with many similar units, its continuity is defined by designation, honors, and historical memory rather than uninterrupted activity in one organizational form.

The 198th Infantry remains part of the Army's larger regimental heritage.
