148th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 148th Infantry Regiment survives in the modern record through its preserved lineage, representing the Army's effort to retain historical continuity across many generations of service.

For regiments of this type, the most important thread is often the continuation of honors, heraldry, and designation rather than an uninterrupted active regimental organization.

The 148th Infantry remains part of the Army's cumulative historical inheritance.
