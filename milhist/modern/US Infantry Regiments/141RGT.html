141st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 141st Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved modern lineages and reflects the way many regimental identities endured through repeated reorganizations of the force.

Its historical identity was often carried through state and federal service, later redesignations, and the retention of lineage and honors even when the regiment was not continuously active as a separate field formation.

The 141st Infantry remains part of the broader institutional heritage preserved in the modern Army.
