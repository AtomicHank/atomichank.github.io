146th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 146th Infantry Regiment forms part of the Army's enduring regimental lineage network, linking modern heraldry to earlier periods of mobilized infantry service.

As with many comparable units, its continuity lies chiefly in preserved lineage and honors, which carried forward even when force structures changed dramatically over time.

The 146th Infantry remains a reminder of the Army's long institutional memory.
