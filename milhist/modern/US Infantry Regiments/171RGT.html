171st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 171st Infantry Regiment survives as a preserved regimental designation in the Army's official lineage system.

Its place in Army history is carried mainly through the continuity of honors and identity across redesignation and reorganization.

The 171st Infantry remains part of the Army's long-term historical record.
