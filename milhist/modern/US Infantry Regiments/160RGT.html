160th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 160th Infantry Regiment is one of the Army's preserved regimental identities, carried into the modern era through formal lineage and honors.

Its history reflects the way unit names, distinctions, and heraldry were maintained even when operational structures were repeatedly revised.

The 160th Infantry remains part of the Army's long institutional memory.
