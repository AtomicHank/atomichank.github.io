136th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 136th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's modern regimental lineage framework and reflects continuity across changing organizational systems. It is one of the many lineages preserved even when not continuously active in full regimental form.

That continuity came through redesignation, honors, and official lineage as the Army adapted to new tactical structures.

The 136th Infantry remains part of the Army's preserved institutional memory.
