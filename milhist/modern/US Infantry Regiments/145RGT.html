145th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 145th Infantry Regiment is part of the Army's preserved regimental framework, maintaining continuity with older state and wartime infantry organizations.

Its lineage reflects the broader pattern of mobilization, demobilization, redesignation, and historical preservation that shaped many twentieth-century infantry regiments.

The 145th Infantry endures primarily through its lineage, honors, and heraldic identity in the modern Army record.
