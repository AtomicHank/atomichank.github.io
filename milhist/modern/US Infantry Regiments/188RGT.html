188th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 188th Infantry Regiment is one of the later regiment numbers retained in Army history through preserved lineage and honors.

Its significance lies in the continuation of official identity and inherited distinction even after structural reorganization.

The 188th Infantry remains part of the Army's maintained regimental tradition.
