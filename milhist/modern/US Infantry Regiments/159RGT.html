159th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 159th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's stored set of regimental lineages, preserving a historical identity that outlived several changes in force structure.

For many units in this range, the most durable thread was the official continuation of honors, campaign credit, and designation.

The 159th Infantry remains part of that preserved institutional continuity.
