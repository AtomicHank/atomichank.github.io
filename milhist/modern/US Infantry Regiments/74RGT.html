74th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 74th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's set of higher-numbered modern lineages used to provide organizational flexibility and reserve depth during the world-war and Cold War periods. It reflects the service's ability to scale beyond the small number of continuously active classic regiments.

Its lineage was sustained through formal honors and redesignations as the Army's tactical organization changed. That continuity preserved the regiment's place in the Army's institutional memory even when it was not a permanently visible field formation.

The 74th Infantry is part of the larger story of how the Army maintained readiness and historical identity at the same time.
