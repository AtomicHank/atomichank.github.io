113th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 113th Infantry Regiment reflects the Army's practice of preserving a wide range of numbered infantry identities through the modern era. Its value lies in organizational continuity and in the maintenance of lineage through changing force structures.

Rather than a single continuous field-regiment story, the 113th Infantry is best understood through redesignation, honors, and the lineage system itself.

That preserved identity keeps the regiment inside the Army's wider historical framework.
