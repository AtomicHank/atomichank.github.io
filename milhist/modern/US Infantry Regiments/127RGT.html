127th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 127th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's modern lineage structure and reflects the preservation of unit identity across mobilization and organizational change. It is part of the broad historical framework that supported the Army's twentieth-century depth.

Its record is chiefly maintained through lineage and honors rather than a continuous field-regiment narrative.

That preserved identity keeps the 127th Infantry within the Army's institutional memory.
