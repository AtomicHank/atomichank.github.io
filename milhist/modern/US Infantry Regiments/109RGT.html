109th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 109th Infantry Regiment is one of the Army's preserved modern-era lineages, representing continuity of identity across periods of mobilization, reorganization, and force reduction. Its place in history lies in that larger system of organizational preservation.

As the Army shifted toward battalion- and brigade-based formations, the 109th Infantry's identity was maintained through lineage and honors rather than through constant regimental field service.

That continuity keeps the regiment within the Army's broader institutional heritage.
