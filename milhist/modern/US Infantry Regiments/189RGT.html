189th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 189th Infantry Regiment belongs to the preserved network of Army regimental lineages that carried older unit identities into the modern era.

Its history is represented mainly through lineage, honors, and designation rather than a single continuous operational structure.

The 189th Infantry remains part of the Army's stored institutional memory.
