104th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 104th Infantry Regiment is one of the higher-numbered infantry lineages preserved within the Army's modern force structure. It shows how the Army retained more unit identities than it could keep continuously active in the field.

Through redesignation and honors, the regiment's name remained part of the service's institutional framework even as tactical organization evolved.

The 104th Infantry therefore contributes to the Army's long organizational continuity in the modern era.
