6th Infantry Regiment Battalions

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

This subpage gathers the battalion insignia associated with the modern 6th Infantry Regiment, the Regulars. The local icon set includes the 1st, 2d, and 3d Battalions, while the public CMH battalion list currently emphasizes the 1st, 2d, and 4th Battalions.

1st Battalion

1st Battalion, 6th Infantry Regiment

The 1st Battalion stands for the regiment's modern armored and mechanized identity. After the 6th Infantry's World War II transformation into armored infantry, battalions such as the 1st Battalion became the practical carriers of the Regulars' lineage inside postwar armored formations, especially those associated with the 1st Armored Division.

2d Battalion

2d Battalion, 6th Infantry Regiment

The 2d Battalion likewise carried the regiment into the mechanized Cold War Army and beyond. Its place in the regiment's story reflects the larger shift by which the old line infantry regiment became a battalion-based lineage serving in armored, mechanized, and expeditionary operations across Europe and later post-Cold War theaters.

3d Battalion

3d Battalion, 6th Infantry Regiment

This icon is preserved here as part of the local battalion image set, but the current public CMH listing for the 6th Infantry Regiment identifies the 1st, 2d, and 4th Battalions rather than a public 3d Battalion page. The section is therefore best read as a heritage placeholder inside the icon set rather than as a fully documented public battalion lineage.

Research note: The 1st and 2d Battalion blurbs are concise summaries based on the regiment's public history and current CMH battalion list checked on 24 March 2026. The 3d Battalion note reflects the public-list mismatch.
