129th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 129th Infantry Regiment is one of the higher-numbered lineages carried within the Army's modern regimental system. Its significance lies in continuity of identity and in the broader administrative depth behind the Army's active field force.

Like many similar regiments, its preserved history comes mainly through lineage and honors rather than through one uninterrupted regimental operational story.

That continuity keeps the 129th Infantry within the Army's long institutional narrative.
