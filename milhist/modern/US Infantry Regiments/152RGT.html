152nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 152nd Infantry Regiment survives in the Army's lineage-and-honors structure, connecting modern heraldry to earlier state and wartime infantry service.

Like many comparable regiments, its continuity is seen chiefly through designation, honors, and memory rather than an uninterrupted independent field career.

The 152nd Infantry remains part of the preserved record of American infantry history.
