93rd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 93rd Infantry Regiment was one of the Army's preserved higher-numbered lineages, part of the broader organizational reserve that made twentieth-century expansion possible. Its history belongs to the modern Army's administrative depth rather than to the older nineteenth-century regular tradition.

As the Army changed its tactical organization, regiments such as the 93rd were often carried forward through lineage, redesignation, and unit honors instead of through continuous regimental field service.

The 93rd Infantry remains part of that wider story of Army continuity across changing structures.
