180th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 180th Infantry Regiment survives in Army lineage records as one of the later-numbered infantry regiments preserved through the modern period.

Its historical presence rests primarily on retained honors, designation, and heraldry rather than uninterrupted active service in one form.

The 180th Infantry remains part of the Army's stored institutional memory.
