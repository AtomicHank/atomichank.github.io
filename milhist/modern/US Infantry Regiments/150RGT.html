150th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 150th Infantry Regiment is preserved as part of the Army's modern lineage structure, reflecting how regimental traditions were retained even as the force was repeatedly reorganized.

Its history fits the broader pattern of many later-numbered infantry regiments: service remembered chiefly through lineage, honors, and institutional continuity rather than through uninterrupted independent activity.

The 150th Infantry remains part of the Army's larger regimental heritage.
