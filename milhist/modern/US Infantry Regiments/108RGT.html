108th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 108th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's higher-numbered modern lineages and reflects the service's need to preserve depth and history across repeated reorganizations. It stands within the same broader system as many of the regiments created or maintained for twentieth-century mobilization.

Its historical profile is thus more institutional than narrative, with identity preserved through lineage and redesignation.

The 108th Infantry remains part of the Army's long administrative and historical memory.
