154th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 154th Infantry Regiment is retained within the Army's lineage framework as part of the larger effort to preserve historic regimental identities.

Its story fits the common pattern of later-numbered infantry regiments whose significance lies in lineage, honors, and inherited tradition.

The 154th Infantry remains part of the Army's cumulative modern heritage.
