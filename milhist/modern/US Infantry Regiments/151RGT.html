151st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 151st Infantry Regiment is preserved within the Army's lineage system as one of the regimental identities carried forward into the modern era.

Its history reflects the larger pattern of mobilization, redesignation, and institutional preservation seen across many later-numbered infantry regiments.

The 151st Infantry remains part of the Army's broader regimental heritage.
