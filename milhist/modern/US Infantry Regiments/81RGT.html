81st Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 81st Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's higher-numbered modern lineages, shaped by wartime expansion and later by the preservation of unit identity through redesignation. It reflects the Army's move toward a larger and more administratively complex force in the twentieth century.

Its history survives primarily through the lineage and honors system rather than through one uninterrupted regimental combat narrative. That makes it representative of many regiments retained for continuity across changing force structures.

The 81st Infantry is therefore part of the Army's broader institutional history of mobilization and preserved identity.
