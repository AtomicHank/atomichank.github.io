173rd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 173rd Infantry Regiment is retained as part of the Army's preserved lineage network, maintaining a historical regimental identity into the modern period.

Its continuity reflects the Army's practice of keeping unit names and honors alive even when active organizations were altered or discontinued.

The 173rd Infantry remains part of the broader record of American infantry service.
