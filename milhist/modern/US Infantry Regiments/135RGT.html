135th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 135th Infantry Regiment belongs to the Army's preserved set of higher-numbered infantry lineages. It reflects the way the service maintained depth, honors, and continuity across periods of mobilization and later reorganization.

Its identity was preserved mainly through the lineage system rather than through one continuous field-regiment career.

The 135th Infantry therefore forms part of the Army's broader institutional narrative in the modern era.
