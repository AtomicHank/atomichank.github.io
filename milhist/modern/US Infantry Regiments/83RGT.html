83rd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 83rd Infantry Regiment belongs to the pool of numbered lineages associated with the Army's twentieth-century expansion and modernization. Its significance lies less in one famous regimental battlefield career than in the preserved organizational identity it contributed to the Army's force structure.

Like many similar regiments, it is best understood through the lineage system that carried names, honors, and identities forward even as tactical formations were reorganized.

The 83rd Infantry remains part of that larger story of continuity inside a changing Army.
