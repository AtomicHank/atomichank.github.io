122nd Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 122nd Infantry Regiment was one of the higher-numbered lineages preserved within the modern Army's infantry system. It reflects the practice of maintaining unit identity across mobilization, redesignation, and later organizational change.

Like many regiments in this range, its significance lies more in continuity than in one uninterrupted independent field-regiment history.

That continuity keeps the 122nd Infantry within the Army's wider institutional heritage.
