69th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 69th Infantry Regiment was one of the numbered lineages preserved within the Army's twentieth-century organizational system. It reflects the service's practice of maintaining additional regimental identities for wartime growth, later specialization, and historical continuity.

Although it did not remain a widely recognized independent field regiment, its lineage still forms part of the institutional record of the modern Army and of the many units created or preserved for large-scale mobilization.

The 69th Infantry belongs to that broader history of Army depth, flexibility, and lineage preservation.
