156th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 156th Infantry Regiment forms part of the Army's enduring lineage structure, preserving an older regimental identity within the modern force.

Its record was carried forward through redesignation and reorganization, a pattern typical of many regiments whose heritage outlasted their original wartime form.

The 156th Infantry remains embedded in the Army's regimental tradition.
