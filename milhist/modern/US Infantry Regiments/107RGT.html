107th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 107th Infantry Regiment was preserved within the Army's modern lineage system as part of its broad roster of infantry identities. Such regiments provided organizational depth and helped carry honors and memory across successive force structures.

Its history is primarily institutional, tied to preservation, redesignation, and continuity rather than one uninterrupted regimental field narrative.

The 107th Infantry remains part of that wider modern Army heritage.
