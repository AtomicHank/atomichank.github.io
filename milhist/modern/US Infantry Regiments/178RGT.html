178th Infantry Regiment

Modern Era, United States, Infantry

The 178th Infantry Regiment forms part of the Army's preserved set of regimental identities carried into the modern era.

Its story is representative of many regiments whose lasting importance lies in lineage, honors, and institutional memory.

The 178th Infantry remains one of the Army's maintained regiment designations.
