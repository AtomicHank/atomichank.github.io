Overview

The KMZ contains 1985 Cold War force placement data organized through a folder hierarchy, custom insignia, and short notes for selected formations. Instead of flattening that into a one-off image, this page preserves the structure so you can browse it geographically and by command chain.

Search for formations by name, click markers to inspect their hierarchy, and use the alliance toggles when you want to isolate one side of the map.
