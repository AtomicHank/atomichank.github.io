Military units387 IAP (40 MiG-23), 1988.
Comments and historyBurevestnik is a PVO interceptor air base that establishes defense of the Kurile Islands. An American DC-8 was forced to land here in 1968 after straying off course. In April 1983 MiG-21s were alerted due to approach of US F-14s but did not take off due to weather. This unit was upgraded with MiG-23 aircraft in 1983. The 387 IAP was disbanded in 1993 and integrated with units at Dolinsk-Sokol and Smyrnykh.
