Comments and historyOlder, small airfield about 11 miles southwest of Olenogorsk. Contains curvy parallel taxiway with a number of small revetments. It was home to the interceptor regiment 174 GV IAP flying MiG-25PDS in the 1980s and as many as 29 MiG-31 in the early 1990s. The 98 ORAP flew the MiG-25RB, Su-17, Su-24MR, and MiG-31.
