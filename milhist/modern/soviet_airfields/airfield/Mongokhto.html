Comments and historyLocated just north of Vanino, this is a major military airfield that has 63 hardened areas. The Navy nuclear wapons storage is either at this airfield or at Maygatka. In 1997 50 family members blocked the runway over back wages. Google Earth showed numerous Tu-95 and Tu-142 aircraft. Full pavement width 80 m but only 40 usable.
