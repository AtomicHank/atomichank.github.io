Provideniya Bay
Alternate names: Urelik,Ureliki
Location: 3kmSW Provideniya, Chukotka, RU
Coordinates: 64°23'N 173°14'W   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 22 m (72 ft)
ICAO:UHMD
Runway:2192 m (7192 ft) (GRAV)
Military units:Civilian

Services small transport aircraft; 4 paved parking spaces. Short gravel airfield probably incapable of military use. Has received Tu-95MR flights.In the spring of 1952, the Soviet Union was operating the TU-4 (a strategic bomber based on a re-engineered B-29 Superfortress) from Mys Shmidta and Provideniya. Avia.ru gives a length of 2000 m and elevation of 16 m.
