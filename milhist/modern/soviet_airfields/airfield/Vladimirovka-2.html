Comments and historyEastern side of Vladimirovka airfield complex. Was a AA missile testing facility c. 1960. During the Cold War it frequently received Tu-160 and Tu-22 flights. Now it is believed to be a scrapyard or depot; contains numerous large aircraft parked haphazardly. Kazakhstan border is 21 km northeast.
