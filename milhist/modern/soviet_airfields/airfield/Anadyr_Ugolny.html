Comments and historyThis is a staging base, operated by OGA (Arctic Control Group), for intercontinental bombers (Tu-22, etc). In 2001 it accepted Tu-95MS and Il-78 aircraft on exercise from Engels. It regularly practices flights to the Canadian arctic. Single parallel taxiway with two 600 x 80 m tarmacs. Civilian parking tarmac can accommodate 25 aircraft.
