Military units:Mixed use. 518 IAP (Tu-128, 1966) (31 MiG-31, 1991)[#1]

Talagi, which opened in 1963, contains considerable tarmac space, very large hangars, and is primarily a civilian airport. Operated Tu-128 in 1970s and early 1980s; interacted with Tu-126 from Siaulyay.
