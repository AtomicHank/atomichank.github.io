Comments and historyThis Aral Sea island was a biowarfare laboratory starting in 1948-49. The runway was expanded in 1980. The facility was closed in 1991 and is now abandoned. Russian documents indicate that plague and anthrax was developed here. The island is rapidly growing due to diminishing lake levels.
