Comments and historyMedium-sized air base near Mikhailovka. Runway reportedly upgraded 1987. Major nuclear bomber base (sister of Khorol). Long runway and curved taxiways with large revetments. Runway width is 60 m but only 40 m is usable. APIB under 1st Air Army Far East.
