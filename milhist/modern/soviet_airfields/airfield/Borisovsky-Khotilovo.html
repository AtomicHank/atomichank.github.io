Borisovsky/Khotilovo
Alternate names: Borisovskiy (US), Borisovsky, Khatilovo
Location: 24kmS Bologoye, Tverskaya, RU
Coordinates: 57°39'N 034°06'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 180 m (591 ft)
Runway:2500 m (8202 ft) (CONC)
Military units:790 IAP (38 MiG-25) (MiG-31, 1991) [#1][#7]

Fighter base with 3 groups of fan revetments. On 1 June 2005 a MiG-31 based here crash-landed here; the crew was only injured.
