Comments and historyKatowice International Airport is an international airport located in Pyrzowice. The airport features two passenger terminals A and B (completed in 2007) and a cargo terminal. Its concrete runway is 2,799 m long and 59 m wide and can accommodate any Boeing 747 or Boeing 777. Heavy transports such as An-124 or An-225 are sometimes seen here.
