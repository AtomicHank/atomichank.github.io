Comments and historyNuclear bomber base. Medium sized interceptor airfield in Baltic region. Its 4 Gv BAP and 63 BAP flew the Yak-28 starting in the 1960s. At some point Tu-16 were based here. It is home to 4 Gv MSHAP flying 29 Su-24 aircraft. The 132 MShAD was based here, as was the 15 ODRAP.
