Comments and historyThe 33rd Air Base (Polish: 33. baza lotnicza) is a Polish Air Force base, located near Powidz. It was officially constituted on 1 January 2000 as the 21st airbase, replacing the disbanded 7th Bomber-Reconnaissance Aviation Regiment, then redesignated to its current name on January 1, 2001. The base has one of the longest runways in Poland. A smaller taxiway serves as a 2750 m runway.
