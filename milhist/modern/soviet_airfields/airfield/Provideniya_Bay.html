Comments and historyServices small transport aircraft; 4 paved parking spaces. Short gravel airfield probably incapable of military use. Has received Tu-95MR flights. In the spring of 1952, the Soviet Union was operating the TU-4 (a strategic bomber based on a re-engineered B-29 Superfortress) from Mys Shmidta and Provideniya.
