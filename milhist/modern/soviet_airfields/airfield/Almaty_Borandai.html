Comments and historyA medium-sized airport suitable for the reception of An-24, An-26, An-30, An-72 and lighter aircraft, as well as helicopters of all types. The owner of the airport is JSC Altair Air. In addition to civil aviation at the airport, government agencies, Border Emergencies Ministry, and Interior Ministry of Kazakhstan are based here.
