Comments and historyDzhemgi is Komsomolsk-na-Amur's northeast side airport, handling small airliner traffic. Small area of fighter revetments. In 2000 the 23 IAP was created (merging 60 IAP Dzemgi and 404 IAP Orlovka). The airfield is home to a large Sukhoi factory. In September 2007 this factory rolled out the new Sukhoi Superjet passenger transport aircraft.
