Comments and historyMedium-size base, 2 parallel taxiways, tarmacs, 12 revetments. It was home to 193 Gv ORAP flying the Yak-28R from the mid-1970s through at least 1991 34 and the MiG-25RB in the 1980s. It is thought to service the Il-76.
