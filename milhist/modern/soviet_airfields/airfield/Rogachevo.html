Comments and historyA major air base, by far the largest on Novaya Zemlya Island. It is under the control of Arctic Control Group for use as a possible staging base for intercontinental bomber flights. It is primarily an interceptor base, home to 641 Gv IAP. It originally flew Yak-28P Firebar interceptors, but upgraded in the 1980s to 32 Su-27 interceptor aircraft. It also had 2 Mi-8 helicopters in 1991. The 641 Gv IAP (Su-27) was transferred from Rogachevo to Afrikanda in September 1993 .
