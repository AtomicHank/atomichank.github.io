Bichye Pole
Alternate names: Kronshtadt (US), Bychje Pole
Location: 36 km W St Petersburg, Leningradskaya, RU
Coordinates: 60°00.8'N 029°42.2'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 3 m (10 ft)

Important spot during WWII. Was a prime site for helicopters and light aircraft in conjunction with the development of the island. (AviaMK)[17]The military now leases it to a small airclub.
