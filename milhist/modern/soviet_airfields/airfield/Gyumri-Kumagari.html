Military units:Mixed. 121 ShAE (5 Su-25, mid 1990s) (2 L-39, mid 1990s).

Services medium-sized airliners. Only 10 km from Turkish border. Terminal area parks 7 aircraft. S-300 antiaircraft missile systems are based here. On 02 Sep 1958 a U.S. Air Force C-130 ELINT plane was shot down here, killing 17.
