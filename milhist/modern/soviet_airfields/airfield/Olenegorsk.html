Military units:Naval Operations Regiment (Tu-95MS, Tu-22M) 1988. 88 OMAPIB (31 MiG-27, 1992)[#3], (9 MiG-23, 1992)[#3]. 924 MRAP (23 Tu-22M, 1992)[#3], (4 Tu-16, 1992)[#3]. 967 ODRAP (Tu-16R). OGA (Arctic Control Group).

Located 92 km south of Murmansk, this is a major bomber airfield with wide runways and taxiways. Serves as a staging base for intercontinental bombers. Nuclear bomber base. Headquarters 5th MRAD. Temporary summer facility for Siaulyay Tu-126.
