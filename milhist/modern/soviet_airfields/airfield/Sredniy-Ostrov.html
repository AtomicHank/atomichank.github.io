Sredniy Ostrov
Location: 893 km N Khatanga, Taymirskaya, RU
Coordinates: 79°31'N 091°04'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 12 m (39 ft)
Runway:4000 m (13123 ft) (?)
Military units:OGA (Arctic Control Group).

Ice airfield used as alternate field for Tu-95 bombers in the Arctic. Created in late 1950s as a staging base for Soviet bombers to reach the U.S. ICAO is UODS. 820 km E of Nagurskoye.
