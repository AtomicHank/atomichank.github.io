Comments and historyHrad?any Airport (Leti?t? Ralsko) is a former military airport in area of town Ralsko in Liberec Region, northern part of the Czech Republic. Built toward the end of World War II for Luftwaffe it was expanded after the war. In 1968 Soviet Army took control and set up large air base here. After the Soviets left in 1991 the airport was abandoned and devastated.
