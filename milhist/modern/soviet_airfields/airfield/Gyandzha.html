Comments and historySignificant military facility with 28 bomber pads (presumably for Tu-16 and Tu-22 type aircraft) and two dozen attack revetments. The runway length does not suggest an intercontinental role. Civilian terminal on west side parks 4 planes. In June 1992 the Azerbaijani military took this base by force from Russia, gaining eleven (western sources say 16) Su-24MRs, 20 MiG-25RBs, and three Il-76 transports. Now is used as a transport base.
