Comments and historyServices medium-size airliners. Appears to be a former bomber staging base. Runway was originally built at 70 m width but only 40 m is usable throughout its length. Considerable tarmac space, with three 400x125m tarmacs and a few alert revetments. Only a few helicopters and An-72 aircraft remain.
