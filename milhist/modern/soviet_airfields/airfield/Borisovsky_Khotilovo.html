Military units790 IAP (38 MiG-25) (MiG-31, 1991) [#1][#7]
Comments and historyFighter base with 3 groups of fan revetments. On 1 June 2005 a MiG-31 based here crash-landed here; the crew was only injured. It appears that the airfield is undergoing major construction. Only a 1550 m runway remained, but numerous MiG-31 aircraft were parked here.
