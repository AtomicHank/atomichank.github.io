Comments and historySmall civilian airfield located on Ostrov Kunashir with one small parking apron. This is the closest airfield to Japan, at a distance of only 50 km. It was built by the Japanese in the 1920s or 1930s. However by 2006 it was closed for rebuilding due to extensive deterioration, and was expected to re-open in 2008. Listed as Sernovodsk on US DoD GNC-6 (1977).
