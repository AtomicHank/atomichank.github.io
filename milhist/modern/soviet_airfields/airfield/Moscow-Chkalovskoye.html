Moscow/Chkalovskoye
Alternate names: Chkalovsky, Star City
Location: 31kmNE Moscow, Moskovskaya, RU
Coordinates: 55°52.8'N 038°03.7'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL

Major transport base AN12/72/Tu154/Il76/IL86VKP. Chkalovsky received USSR's first Il-76K for cosmonaut training 23 July 1977. May be confused with Kaliningrad/Chklalovsk or Omsk/Chkalovsk.
