Comments and historyWestern WWII Lend-Lease ALSIB route terminus. The site is quite specific this location in northeast Krasnoyarsk is the ALSIB terminus location. As of 2004 airplanes and pads were still noted at N56 02.44 E92 55.40. The airfield is long destroyed and has been replaced by high-rise apartments. Airfield geometry and position validated using declassified Corona imagery (DZB00403600007H001002 1967/02/03 4036 00007).
