Norilsk (Alykel)
Location: 35 km W Norilsk, Krasnoyarski krai, RU
Coordinates: 69°18.7'N 087°20.1'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 175 m (574 ft)
ICAO:UOOO
Runway:3441 m (11289 ft) (CONC)
Military units:Mixed use. 57 IAP (24 Su-15TM, 1991)[#1]. OGA (Arctic Control Group).

Alykel is the only functional airfield around Norilsk. It handles medium-sized airliners; 24 hour operations. Designated as emergency airfield for cross-polar route. Created in late 1950s as staging base for Soviet bombers to reach the U.S.
