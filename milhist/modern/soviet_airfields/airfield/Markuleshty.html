Comments and historyMajor Moldovian Air Force Base. The MiG-29 aircraft were provided by Russia in 1992. Due to high maintenance and operations costs, the MiG-29s were sold to the U.S. in 1997, which sent C-17 jets that October to airlift them to the U.S.
