Uzin (Chepelevka)
Alternate names: Uzin-Sherpelovka, Uzyn (US)
Location: 4kmS Uzin, Kievskaya, UA
Coordinates: 49°47'N 030°26'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 173 m (568 ft)
Runway:3500 m (11483 ft) (CONC)
Military units:409 TBAP (Tu-95, 1955)[#6]. 1006 TBAP (Tu-95 1956-)[#2] (Tu-95K 1957-) (Tu-95MS 1985-) (21 Tu-95, 1991, 25 in 1994)[#2]. 409 APSZ (Tu-95 1955-) (Il-78 1986-1991)[#2]. 182 TBAP (Tu-95).

Medium-size bomber base. Received Russia's first Tu-95 in 1955 (409 TBAP)[#6]. The 182 TBAP (Tu-95) moved here from Mozdok, then to Russia.
