Comments and historyA combat airfield with numerous hardened aircraft shelters. It was home to 118 OAPREB flying Yak-28Rs in the mid 1970s 34 and the 118 ORAP flying Su-24MR in the early 1990s. Part of the airfield is being used for industrial storage.
