Comments and historyImportant airfield during WWII especially during the war with Finland. Il-2 aircraft were typically based here. It was a prime site for helicopters and light aircraft in conjunction with the development of the island and is operated by the Leningrad naval military district. (AviaMK)[17] The military now leases it to a small airclub.
