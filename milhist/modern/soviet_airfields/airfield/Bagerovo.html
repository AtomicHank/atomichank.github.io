Comments and historyFrom 1947 to 1972 Bagerovo was a 71st VVS (12 GUMO) airfield with nuclear bombers and units 35 BAP, 513 IAP, and 647 SAPSO. The first cruise missiles were tested here. It was designated as an emergency landing strip for the Buran. The airfield is now in ruins.
