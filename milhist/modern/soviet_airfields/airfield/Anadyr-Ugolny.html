Anadyr/Ugolny
Alternate names: Leninka (US), Ugolnyye Kopi, Ugolnoye
Location: 11kmE Anadyr, Chukotka, RU
Coordinates: 64°44'N 177°44'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 59 m (194 ft)
ICAO:UHMA
Runway:3500 m (11483 ft) (CONC)
Military units:OGA (Arctic Control Group).

This is a staging base for intercontinental bombers (Tu-22, etc). In 2001 it accepted Tu-95MS and Il-78 aircraft on exercise from Engels. It regularly practices flights to the Canadian arctic. Single parallel taxiway with two 600 x 80 m tarmacs.Civilian parking tarmac can accommodate 25 aircraft.
