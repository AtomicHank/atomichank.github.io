Comments and historyThis airfield has served primarily as an AWACS operations base for the Arctic regions. The airfield has about 12 hardstands, small tarmacs. The runway is only marked out to 2850 m with a 150 m threshold. The 144 OAPDRLO flew the A-50 Mainstay starting in 1989.
