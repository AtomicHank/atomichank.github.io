Kaliningrad/Chkalovsk
Alternate names: Tchalov, Proveren (US)
Location: 9kmNW Kaliningrad, Kaliningradskaya, RU
Coordinates: 54°46'N 020°24'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 50 m (164 ft)

Military units:846 OMSHAP (44 Su-17, 1992)[#3]. 15 ODRAP (12 Su-24, 1992)[#3].

Kaliningrad's largest airfield, a significant military base with four separate complexes for bomber and fighter parking. May be confused with Chkalovsk in Moscow and Omsk.
