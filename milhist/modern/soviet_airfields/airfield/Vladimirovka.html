Vladimirovka
Alternate names: Vladmirovka,Akhtubinsk
Location: 6kmNE Akhtubinsk, Astrakhanskaya, RU
Coordinates: 48°19'N 046°14'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 15 m (49 ft)
Runway:4000 m (13123 ft) (CONC)

Large airfield complex (actually two airfields connected together). Western runway 2500m, eastern 4000m. Airfield is 30SE Kapustin Yar. Was a AA missile testing facility c. 1960. Kazakhstan border is 21 km northeast.
