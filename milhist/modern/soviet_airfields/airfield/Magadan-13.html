Handles most prop transports. Regular passenger Il-14 were flying here in 1950s-60s. In 70s-80s it was a busy airport for charter operations (An-2, Mi-4, Mi-8, etc) that served geological expeditions, firefighters, and local routes.Currently there are An-2s, Mi-8s, and a helicopter maintenance facility.
