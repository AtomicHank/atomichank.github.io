Riga/Spilve
Alternate names: Tsentralny (US)
Location: 5 km N Riga, Latvia, LV
Coordinates: 56°59'N 024°05'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 22 m (72 ft)
Runway:1650 m (5413 ft) (CONC)
Military units:Abandoned

Former 1950s-era military base, now unused. Ring taxiway with small revetments. High-res sat only shows two general-aviation planes parked. Large Technical School until 90's with one of each major aircraft type, most broken up around 96/7 (AviaMK).
