Comments and historyMajor combat air base with numerous reveted areas. In 1965 it was home to Li-2, An-2, Il-14, Mi-4, Il-18, An-10, and Tu-104 jet. By the mid-1970s it was operating only the An-2 and An-12. It was at one time examined for a Berlin airport expansion plan.
