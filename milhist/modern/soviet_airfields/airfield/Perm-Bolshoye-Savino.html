Location: 16kmSW Perm, Permskaya, RU

Military units:Mixed use. 764 IAP (38 MiG-25, 1991) (MiG-31, 1991)[#1].

Mixed use airfield with small number of fighter and bomber pads. Services medium-sized airliners, with 39 parking spots near terminal and cargo area. During Cold War operated MiG-25, Yak-25, Yak-28. Received MiG-31s in 1991.Length given as 2500 m on a 1998 Jeppesen.
