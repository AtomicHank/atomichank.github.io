Ivanovo/Severnii
Alternate names: Ivanovo North (US), Zhukovka
Location: 6kmN Ivanovo, RU
Coordinates: 57°03.5'N 040°58.9'E   Google TerraServer Multimap GeoURL
Elevation: 137 m (449 ft)
Runway:2300 m (7546 ft) (CONC)
Military units:81 VTAP (Il-76, An-12, An-22, disbanded 1998)[#4] 2457 DRLO (A-50)[#7]

Large transport operations airfield with hangars and significant tarmac space. A civilian airfield with 2500 m runway exists 7 km S of Ivanovo. Runway built 1935, upgraded 1965. Received Russia's first Il-76 delivery 3 June 1974.NE of Ivanovo. An22 reworks (former base) IL76 AN12 base with most VVS A50(IL76) AWACs based Small military Museum (AviaMK)
