Comments and historyAirfield appears to have been in much better condition in the 1950s with minor concrete runway; this runway is in poor condition now and is filled in with gravel in many areas. Only about 1150 of hard surface exist on the 1600 m runway. Several small maintenance buildings exist on the airfield.
