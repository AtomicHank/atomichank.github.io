Comments and historyIn 1965 the airfield had MiG-17, MiG-21, Yak-12, and MiG-15UTI, but these were replaced with MiG-21 in the mid-1970s. The 487 OVP operated here in the 1990s flying Mi-8 and Mi-24. The last Russian regiment departed in April 1994.
