Comments and historyHandles small transport aircraft. Paved runway and one small tarmac. This was part of the Nome-Provideniya (questionable) or Uel-Kal-Markovo-Seymchan-Yakutsk leg of the WWII Lend-Lease program. It was usually used by P-39 fighters enroute to Seymchan to refuel since there were no other airfields on this stretch. A 2428 m length comes from an unknown source.
