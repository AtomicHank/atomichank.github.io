Comments and historyIn 1965 the base had MiG-17, Su-7 or Su-17, Yak-12, and MiG-15UTI. It was operating only the Yak-28 by the 1970s. A MiG-23 regiment returned to Russia in March 1993. Only a 1200 x 30 m subset of the original runway is in use.
