Overview

Building things for real changes how you think. Materials resist, tolerances matter, and every shortcut leaves evidence somewhere.

This section is for practical project work across construction, woodworking, and metalworking, with room to branch into other fabrication topics later.

Construction

General building methods, tools, planning, and the realities of working on larger physical projects where sequence and fit matter.

Woodworking

Joinery, material prep, finishing, shop workflow, and the particular satisfaction of turning rough stock into something precise.

Metalworking

Cutting, shaping, fastening, and fabrication methods for projects that need strength, accuracy, or a different set of tools entirely.
