The eternal question...why? Why does anyone collect information on a website and make it accessible to the world at large? I've seen many websites throughout the years and for the most part, it seems the hobbyist programmers wanted to nerd out and program a website. So, they chose another hobby, usually just as nerdy, organized it into 0s and 1s, and threw it out there.

As I ask myself why I am doing this, I come up with much the same answer. I've spent more years than I care to remember trying to learn web programming and wanting to try my hand at creating content. I endeavor to not only put information out there, but also action that information.

For example, you will find a section on land navigation as a part of the maps section. I present the “academic theory” of land navigation, but also couple it with YouTube videos explaining it, walking through the steps, and actually doing it.

Though I don’t consider myself a “content creator”, nor do I plan on making money from this, I would like to create and catalog information regarding my various hobbies and interests. Why? Maybe for posterity’s sake, maybe because I have hard drives full of rando information I’ve collected over three decades of surfing the internet and want an excuse to organize it. Maybe for the best reason of all… just because!


Military History

Military history deals with man's progress in warfare from ancient conflicts to modern warfare, thus indicating strategies, technologies, and individuals who have shaped human civilization. Military history is a discipline that maps tactics and innovations; it is the influence of historical moments through the causes and effects of wars and their profound impact on societies. Through military history, one can trace all of the complexities in how geopolitical dynamics have changed over time, how military technology has evolved through the ages, and how the human spirit has remained indomitable, carrying humanity through conflict after conflict—all together offering a holistic understanding as to how past military events have charted a course that continues to influence the present and future.



American Culture

American culture is a dynamic tapestry of diverse influences, reflecting the two-century-long history of immigration and innovation that has typified the country. It has elements from various traditions of Native American, European, African, Asian, and Latin American cultures that combine to make it unique and dynamic. Some of the essential constituents of U.S. culture are individualism, freedom, and democracy, as well as a fondness for popular culture, sports, and technology. American culture is celebrated for the ingenuity it shows in its music, film, and art forms and by the spirit of entrepreneurship and progress, insuring change is always constant and fascinating.
