Nerd Stuff Overview

Welcome to the intersection of technology, science, and passionate hobbies. This section explores the technical pursuits and geeky interests that drive innovation, creativity, and endless curiosity about how things work.

Computers

Dive into the world of computing, from hardware architecture to software development. Explore programming languages, system administration, and the evolution of personal computing technology.

Electronics

Understanding circuits, components, and electronic systems. From basic resistors and capacitors to complex microcontroller projects and embedded systems design.

Math

The language of science and engineering. Explore mathematical concepts, problem-solving techniques, and the beautiful patterns that govern our universe through numbers and equations.

Amateur Radio

The art and science of radio communication. Learn about frequencies, antennas, propagation, and the global community of ham radio operators connecting across the airwaves.

Models

Scale modeling brings history and imagination to life. From plastic aircraft to detailed dioramas, discover the craftsmanship and attention to detail that define this hobby.

Model Railroading

Creating miniature worlds of transportation. Explore layout design, scenery techniques, and the technical aspects of running model trains in realistic settings.

Lego

The ultimate building system for creativity and engineering. From simple brick constructions to complex Technic mechanisms and custom MOCs (My Own Creations).
